# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is D.A.V.I.D AI?

D.A.V.I.D AI (Digital Assistant with Voice & Intelligent Decisions) is an advanced, privacy-first AI assistant for Android that combines voice control, gesture recognition, and intelligent conversation capabilities.

### Who developed D.A.V.I.D AI?

D.A.V.I.D AI is developed by **Nexuzy Tech Ltd.**, a company focused on building privacy-first AI applications.

**Contact**: [david@nexuzy.in](mailto:david@nexuzy.in)

### Is D.A.V.I.D AI free?

Yes! D.A.V.I.D AI is completely free and open-source.

### Does it collect my data?

**NO!** D.A.V.I.D AI does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything happens locally on your device.

## Installation & Setup

### What Android version do I need?

Android 8.0 (API 26) or higher.

### How much storage do I need?

- **Low-end devices**: ~914 MB
- **Mid-range devices**: ~1.6 GB
- **High-end devices**: ~2.7 GB

### How long does model download take?

2-5 minutes depending on your device and internet connection.

### Can I use it without internet?

Yes! After initial model download, D.A.V.I.D AI works completely offline.

### Why does it need so many permissions?

Each permission is used for specific features:
- Camera: Gesture control
- Microphone: Voice commands
- Storage: Storing AI models
- Phone/SMS: Voice-activated calls/messages
- Location/WiFi/Bluetooth: Device control

All permissions are used locally only.

## Gesture Control

### How do I use gesture control?

Simply show your hand to the camera. The app will detect it automatically.

### What gestures are supported?

- Open Palm: Show pointer
- Closed Fist: Hide pointer
- Pointing Up: Move pointer
- Victory Sign: Click
- Thumbs Up: Confirm

### Why isn't gesture recognition working?

1. Check camera permission
2. Ensure good lighting
3. Keep hand clearly visible
4. Check if gesture models downloaded
5. Try adjusting camera angle

## AI Models

### What AI models does it use?

- **Voice**: Whisper (from HuggingFace)
- **Chat**: TinyLlama/Qwen/Phi-2

## Performance

### Why is the app slow?

1. AI processing is resource-intensive
2. Ensure sufficient RAM (2GB+ recommended)
3. Close background apps
4. Restart device
5. Try lighter models

### Does it drain battery?

Background services (voice/gesture detection) use some battery. You can disable them when not needed.

### Why is it using so much storage?

AI models are large files (1-3GB). This is normal for on-device AI.

## Troubleshooting

### App crashes on startup

1. Check Android version (8.0+ required)
2. Ensure sufficient RAM
3. Clear app cache
4. Reinstall app

### Models won't download

1. Check internet connection
2. Ensure sufficient storage
3. Grant storage permission
4. Try different network
5. Restart app

### Voice commands not working

1. Grant microphone permission
2. Check voice models downloaded
3. Reduce background noise
4. Speak clearly
5. Try rephrasing command

### Gestures not recognized

1. Grant camera permission
2. Ensure good lighting
3. Keep hand visible
4. Check gesture models downloaded
5. Adjust camera angle

For more help, see [Troubleshooting Guide](Troubleshooting).
